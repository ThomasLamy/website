Research

Most of my current research is based in the Western Indian Ocean, particularly in Madagascar, and is supported by two major projects: ARMS Resilience in Madagascar and MESCAL, which spans the eastern coast of Africa, including South Africa, Mozambique, Tanzania, and Kenya. For more details on my Projects Page.
